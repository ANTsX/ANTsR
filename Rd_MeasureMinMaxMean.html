MeasureMinMaxMean¶

purpose:

Measure Min Max Mean

description:

Measure min, max, and mean of image.

usage:

MeasureMinMaxMean(image)

examples:

myimg <- as.antsImage(array(rnorm(1000), dim = c(10, 10, 10)))

MeasureMinMaxMean(myimg)
