=========================
matrixToImages 
=========================

purpose:  

 Convert rows of a matrix to images
 
description:  

   Unmasks rows of a matrix and writes as images.
 

usage:  

 matrixToImages(dataMatrix, mask, outputRoot)
 
examples: 

.. sourcecode:: r
    

    mat = matrixToImages(aMat, mask, "aMat_")
